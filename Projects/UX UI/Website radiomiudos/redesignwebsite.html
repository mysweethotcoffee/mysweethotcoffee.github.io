Redesign of a website

Concept

For this project we had to redesign an existing website of our choice that we considered bad in terms of usability and acessibility for users to use. We were a duo and we choose the website radiomiudos.pt, because of the bad usability the website has, since it is pretty hard to find something or navegate in it.

First step

The first thing to do was to see everything we though was necessary to change for a better usability, and after that we drawed sketches of what we wanted the wireframes to look like.

Wireframes

With the wireframes created, the next step would be to create the first version of digital wireframes, and see what it would look like.

Final Result

With all of the wireframes ready, we started the design of it, altering some details in the wireframes while on it.
