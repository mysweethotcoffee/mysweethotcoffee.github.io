Video project and/or Motion Design

Concept

My concept for this project is based on representing small moments of a character called Sebastião, a chungus cat, in his fun routine.

Chungus - a name given to chubby cats

Target Audience

This project does not have a specific target audience in terms of age. On the other hand, it also targets people who are lovers of furry and chubby felines.

Initial Process

First Sketches

Having the concept and idea defined, I started to create some sketches from the ideas, later focusing on just three main drafts to create three animations. In addition, I made some illustrations of the cat to create physical stickers.

Process - Storyboards

Consecutively, I started creating storyboards for the animations, and, in the end, create animatics with them.

Process - Illustration

With the storyboard finished, the last step would be to create the animation frame per frame and exporte it.
